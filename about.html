Our Mission

The mission of the New England Gladiators Basketball Program is to provide the youth of Lawrence, MA, and beyond the opportunity to learn and enjoy basketball at a highly competitive level. Our program aims to develop individual and team skills, promote sportsmanship, team spirit, and organizational loyalty. We demand a high degree of ethical conduct from our coaches, players, volunteers, and parents, setting a positive example for the community. By cooperating with local youth programs and schools, we enhance our players' opportunities to excel at the middle school, high school level, and beyond. Our goal is to offer a fun basketball experience while teaching valuable life lessons such as teamwork, problem-solving, loyalty, discipline, hard work, and how to win.
